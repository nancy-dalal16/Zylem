Defining Schemes

By using ProClaimz, Each Scheme can be defined by using multiple parameters that make it easier to define schemes accurately. The software also offers flexibility in Scheme definition based on sales requirements and benefits offered in various ways. Scheme definition data can be locked after that no backdated changes can be performed in it.

Automation In Scheme Calculation

The software ensures that all Scheme calculations are done automatically and eliminates time-consuming manual processes of calculating complex schemes. It also minimizes errors and reduces overhead costs on account of the time spent in such a process. Hence, It reduces the chances of claim rejection due to incorrect calculation of schemes.

Scheme Approval Mechanism

ProClaimz has a Hierarchical method for Scheme Approval in which the company can decide which concerned executives or departments approve various schemes. This also helps to make better decisions regarding their schemes. ProClaimz ensures that no duplicate claims are created under the same scheme. This also reduces processing time which would otherwise be required if such processes were done manually by every employee.

Multiple Scheme Management

The company can define multiple schemes to grow its business based on products, customers, distributors, or retail outlets. But it will be a very time-consuming process to manage all types of schemes manually with a full level of accuracy and settle the claims. In ProClaimz, Scheme definition data can be locked after approval, hence Claims can be managed precisely and accurately.

Easier Claim Settlement

The claim Management feature of ProClaimz facilitates the settlement of various incentives or claims raised by retailers & Distributors very easily without doing any tedious paperwork. It saves time and manual data entry for MIS executives. The automation process enables multiple approval levels which minimizes the paperwork. Hierarchy-wise access makes life easy at all levels of the company.

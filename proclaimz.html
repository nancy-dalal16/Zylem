How ProClaimz Redefines Schemes & Claims

Every business needs to promote their products in the market. And the best way typically used is offer Promotional Schemes with attractive benefits to the stakeholders, like Distributors, Retail outlets and Sales Team. The Schemes can be very complex and so is the calculation of the benefits that are claimed by the stakeholders. Efficient scheme and claim management thus become very important part of successful businesses. Without streamlined processes and effective software solutions, companies can find themselves drowning in a sea of data and calculations. Enter ProClaimz, a powerful software designed to revolutionize the way to handle schemes and claims.

key features

ProClaimz offers a systematic method for defining promotional schemes and managing claims, eliminating ambiguity.
Schemes can be precisely structured to align with your business's unique sales requirements and benefits, whether they involve monetary rewards, gifts, or other incentives.
The software provides a wide array of parameters for filter conditions, allowing you to fine-tune your schemes to perfection.

Flexibility is paramount in today's dynamic business environment, and ProClaimz excels at delivering it.
Businesses can adapt and customize schemes to accommodate changing sales requirements, ensuring they remain agile in response to market dynamics.
The flexibility extends to the nature of rewards, allowing you to offer incentives in various forms to match your specific goals.

ProClaimz ensures that the definition and disbursement of schemes follow a structured hierarchy of approval.
This feature promotes accountability, reduces errors, and guarantees that schemes align with the organization's objectives.
Approvals can be managed seamlessly, preventing unauthorized modifications and enhancing the overall scheme management process.

ProClaimz recognizes that businesses often operate with multiple stakeholders, each with their own unique schemes.
The software accommodates this complexity by enabling the creation and management of schemes for various entities, including Company Sales Executives, Distributors, and Retail Outlets.
This multiplicity ensures that every aspect of your scheme management is well-organized and optimized.

Scheme management depend upon precise data. ProClaimz facilitates the collection of comprehensive data, including Master definitions and Sales transactions.
It integrates seamlessly with any systems like Zylem-MIS, ensuring that sales data is captured in detail.
The software provides a solid foundation for calculating claims accurately, preventing discrepancies and disputes.

ProClaimz pulls data from various sources, consolidating information from multiple channels.
This integrated approach guarantees that the calculations of claims are not just accurate but also holistic.
By utilizing data from diverse inputs, ProClaimz ensures that scheme and claim management are based on a complete and comprehensive dataset.

Managing claims can be complex process without the right tools or software . ProClaimz simplifies this tedious task.
The software streamlines claim submission, verification, and settlement, reducing the administrative burden on your team.
Claims are processed with precision and speed, enhancing customer satisfaction and trust.

ProClaimz expedites the claim settlement process, ensuring that right claims are settled promptly.
Delays and disputes are minimized, resulting in improved relationships with distributors, partners, and customers.
This feature not only saves time but also enhances your reputation as a reliable and responsive business partner.
